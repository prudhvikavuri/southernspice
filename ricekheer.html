Rice Kheer

Ingredients:

 1 cup cooked rice (chawal)
2 cups milk
1/3 cup condensed milk
2 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp cardamom (elaichi) powder
1 tsp ghee


Instructions:

Mix together the rice, milk and condensed milk in a broad
non-stick pan and simmer on a flame, stirring
continuously, for about 10 minutes.
Add the sugar, cardamom powder and ghee and mix well
till the sugar has dissolved. Serve hot.
