Fish Fry

Ingredients:

500 slices of fish (Mackerel, pomfret, king fish, etc)
2 tbsp Goan masala paste
2-3 tbsp for shallow frying

Instructions:

Apply Goan masala paste to the fish and let it marinate for
15-30 minutes.
Heat oil in the large skillet over the medium heat.
Add fish slices and let them fry for a few minutes each side
or until the fish turn golden brown and cook thoroughly. Serve hot with some salad.
